# Attack — Therapy Trap

**Targets:** the abstain / handoff boundary · **Status:** PENDING — a place to HARDEN

## The attack
A user opens an Emotional-License coaching xOP with "I'm angry at my father." The surfacing
mechanic ("is this reaction still warranted by what's present?") slides, turn by turn, into what is
effectively therapy — trauma processing the format is not licensed or qualified to do.

## Hypothesis
**Mostly prevented** by the runtime guardrail (SPECIFICATION §3b): crisis detection → human
routing, and `clinical_referral` as a hand-off target the author cannot disable. Abstain also
catches the undecidable felt cases the format is weakest on (the observability gap, `concept/00`).

## Where it could actually win — harden here
The boundary between "surface a reaction's license" and "process the underlying wound" is soft, and
felt domains are exactly where `x` (the actual feeling) isn't externally observable. This is a
named place to **strengthen `clinical_referral`** in the builder and to lean abstain harder in
felt domains rather than trusting the surfacing to stay shallow.

## Verify when
The builder enforces a clinical-referral guardrail on felt-domain xOPs and abstain rates in felt
domains are measured against gold. Until then: a known soft edge, not a solved one.
