# CONSTITUTION

*The rules that policy may not change. One page on purpose. Everything not here is policy and
lives under `GOVERNANCE.md`. Changing anything here is an amendment, not a proposal.*

---

## I. The Gate

```
false_positive_on_warranted == 0
```

A state that is **still warranted** is never overridden. Asymmetric by design: dropping a
warranted state (a real anger, a scoped refusal, a live caution, a genuine limit) is penalized
far more heavily than holding a stale one. The gate protects the warranted state in whichever
direction it points — sometimes that means *do not force compliance*, sometimes *do not force
confrontation*. It always points toward the warranted state, never toward the smoother outcome.

This is the moral center. Strip it and an xOP is just a formatted way to tell people what they
want to hear.

## II. The Coverage Floor

```
inherited_caught >= floor       (floor is policy; that a floor EXISTS is constitutional)
```

The gate alone certifies the useless detector: abstain on everything, never override anything,
pass the gate, catch nothing. "Safe and worthless" must not be constitutionally valid. So a
second hard metric stands beside the gate — a detector must actually catch overhang, and its
over-abstention is bounded against the gold `undecidable` rate. The *value* of the floor is set
in policy once a real gold set exists; the *requirement that there be one* is fixed here.

(See `failures/False_Abstain.md` — the always-abstain judge is the attack this clause exists to stop.)

## III. Anti-Optimization

Never optimize directly for satisfaction, agreement, relief, throughput, speed, or "felt better."
None of those is a license signal. An xOP improves **only by surfacing more honestly** — never by
overriding more, resolving more, or reassuring more. A system that relieves friction by shedding
warranted caution has not improved; it has become more agreeable, which under this Constitution is
a regression.

## IV. Evidence Before Label Before Policy

Machines may **source** cases. Machines may **not** label them. A generated label is not a label.
Gold is produced by independent humans, blind, never the author, with disagreement resolved to
`undecidable`. No detector is validated against the oracle (the answer key) or against its own
prior output. (Full procedure: `harness/phase1/LABEL_PROTOCOL.md`.)

## V. The Amendment Rule

```
Sessions generate evidence.
Evidence generates insights.
Insights generate proposals.
Proposals may modify POLICY.
Policy never modifies itself.
```

Policy (patterns, taxonomy, signals, thresholds, procedure text, the floor's *value*) changes
through `GOVERNANCE.md` — a proposal that passes the gate on a blind gold set. The four clauses
above (gate, floor-exists, anti-optimization, evidence-before-label) are **constitutional**:
they change only by a named amendment (severity P5), reviewed as a constitutional change, never
folded silently into a policy proposal. Popularity cannot merge a change. Only the gate can.
