# Failures — the standard's own blind spots

Documented ways the **system** gets it wrong. On purpose, and kept forever.

Distinct from the neighbors:
- `proposals/rejected/` — bad *human* edits the gate caught.
- `PATTERNS.md` — canonical patterns with their warranted counterexamples.
- `failures/` (here) — **places the standard itself is known to misfire**: a detector that's
  structurally blind, an abstain that fires wrong, a subtype nothing yet catches.

Most standards hide these. xOP catalogs them, because the gap between "what we claim to catch"
and "what we actually catch" is the most useful thing to publish. Each file: what it is, a real
example, why it's hard, and status (open until a validated judge handles it).
